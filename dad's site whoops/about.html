Mission Statement

Zhai & Wang, LLP is a Silicon Valley based tax, accounting and financial advisory related professional service firm. It specializes in small and mid-size company’s accounting, tax return preparation, tax planning, and financial statement compilation, review and auditing. No matter it is for helping start-ups acquire venture funding, bank loan or rendering continuous controllership advisory service, Zhai & Wang, LLP has extensive experience in the areas. With strong knowledge in tax planning and systematic approaches in financial planning, Zhai & Wang, LLP enhances its clients’ high net worth through well design practices like wealth accumulation, assets protection, tax avoidance and financial advisory.

Firm Profile

Zhai & Wang, LLP is a full service Certified Public Accounting firm licensed in California. Because of the diverse background of our partners, the firm is able to offer a wide range of services:

  • Accounting Write-up (bookkeeping with financial statement preparation)
  • Payroll
  • Tax planning and Preparation
  • Real-time Accounting
  • Entity Formation
  • Financial Reporting and Analysis
  • International Tax Disclosure

About the Team

Bin Zhai

Bin Zhai, CFP®, CPA, MBA, Partner

A practitioner for over 20 years, helping dozens of small companies from startup to growth. Mr. Zhai specializes in tax planning and preparation; company formation; accounting information system implementation; and financial performance consulting. He starts his career in public accounting firms in California, including working with Mohler, Nixon and Williams, ranking as the fifth largest accounting firm in the Silicon Valley. Bin Zhai received his bachelor's degree in Accounting in 1989 from Shanghai University of Finance and Economics in China, and earned his MBA degree concentration on Accountancy from California State University, Fullerton in 1993.

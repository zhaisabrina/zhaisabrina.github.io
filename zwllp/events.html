Seminars

"2018 Tax Reform Impact to Business Owners"

Date: Friday, June 29, 2018
Time: 12:30PM-01:30PM
Address:
Conference Room, Zhai & Wang LLP
2051 Junction Ave Ste 200,
San Jose, CA 95131

Description:

With the kicking start of 2018 tax season, the new tax reform - Tax Cuts and Jobs Act (TCJA) passed by Congress and signed by president Trump is starting to take effect from January 1, 2018. The depth and breadth of this new tax law change have never been seen in more 30 years since 1986 tax reform. However, our firm has developed a unique niche practice to handle these complicated cases for many clients with this kind international background. This year, we invite the tax expert, Mr. Bin Zhai, CPA, CFP® from Zhai & Wang, LLP, to host a tax seminar to help business owner understand new tax laws impact to their business.

  1. Could your taxes go up?
  2. What will your 2018 tax obligation be under the new law?
  3. What business deductions or exemptions have been created?
  4. How will this impact your business?
  5. Is there an opportunity to restructure my business to a better position for tax breaks?
  6. Which companies are likely to benefit from a lower tax rate?
To RSVP, please fill out the form below or contact us at (408) 383-9870x0.

Accounting Services

Any company planning for business success must rely on its ability to analyze its financial data. History and experience prove financial statements are still the ultimate source for major financial data. When accurately prepared financial statements are provided to you in a clear and timely manner, they become the basis for a deeper understanding of your financial position.

Zhai & Wang, LLP will work with you not only to prepare timely financial statements, but will work with you to devise strategies that can improve your financial condition. Whether you require accurate financial reporting for the needs of internal analysis, start-up investment or as part of bank loan, our professionals will provide a full range of accounting and financial reporting services which include:

  • Preparation of compiled financial statements.
  • Tax preparation for individuals, business and trust/estates at federal and multi-state levels
  • Monthly write-up services with bookkeeping and financial statement preparation
  • Real time accounting outsourcing services
  • Monthly financial package preparation
  • Projected financial statements
  • Cash flow projection and analysis
  • Financial operating results analysis

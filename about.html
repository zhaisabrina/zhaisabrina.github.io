Hello!



Interaction Designer. HCI. Coder.

I'm Sabrina, a second year student in the School of Design at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm currently pursuing a double major in Human-Computer Interaction, which combines my interests in computer science with design, in hopes of creating better tech for people. I want to create design that is centered around people, specifically finding ways that technology can better serve us, making life easier and comfortable to thrive in. I enjoy discovering the ways that decisions designers make can impact the user, and how these interactions plays a greater role in how our lives run.

Outside of design, my interests include illustration and animation, reading, and finding tea shops with a lot of sunlight!

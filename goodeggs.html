Branded Experiences: Pop-Up Shop

Brand research and analysis, Strategy, Marketing
SketchUp, 3D Printing, Laser Cutting, Rapid Prototyping, Illustration
Design Context

Design a pop-up shop for the company Good Eggs, a Bay-Area online grocery service that focuses on fresh, local produce delivered straight to the customer.

Timeline

Part 1 (2 weeks): Brand Research, Key Insights
Part 2 (6 weeks): Iterations, Rapid Prototyping
Part 3 (2 weeks): Marketing Strategy

Final Deliverables

Physical Model
Digital Model
Site Renders


00 / Final Deliverables












01 / Research

From the brand research I conducted with Meijie Hu, we concluded three key insights. We also focused on the competitor market, so we can evluate where our brand fits within these competitors.













02 / Pop-up experience

After researching the brand image of Good Eggs, I began to ideate what kind of experience I want to create. I looked back into my insights, and tried to encapsulate the feelings of what I investigated. I decided with the idea that Good Eggs provides its customers with a health lifestyle track, with no effort needed on their part.

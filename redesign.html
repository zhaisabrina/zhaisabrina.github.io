User Research, Visual and UX Design, User Testing
Design Context

At my 10 week internship at TigerGraph, a graph analytics startup, I served as the UI/UX designer on the front-end development team. I mainly worked on the platform, GraphStudio, This software makes analyzing and understanding big data simpler, more efficient, and more effective. It is used by companies such as Intuit, Wish, and Zillow.

My role & contribution

Currently, GraphStudio is a program that is targeted towards technical users and in my redesign, I evaluated different user needs from both technical and non-technical, so that the target audience can be expanded and used by more employees within a company.
